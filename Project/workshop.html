Home decoration


Showroom inauguration decorations, wedding anniversaries, birthday parties, and Valentine's Day are among the special events where flowers are decorated. Depending on the situation, different flowers represent different things. Some flowers are used as symbols for love, serenity, purity, beauty, and more.

Flower Arrangement


Flower arrangement is an art in which an attempt is made to arrange flowers , foliage ,plants and flower vases in such a way that all of these when arranged produce a harmonious appearance, texture and color combination.

Flower Care


Floristry is the production, commerce, and trade in flowers. It encompasses flower care and handling, floral design and arrangement, merchandising, production, display and flower delivery.

Flower Group

A group or cluster of flowers formed on a stem made of a primary branch or a sophisticated arrangement of branches is known as an inflorescence. It can also be defined as a plant's reproductive part that bears a cluster of flowers in a certain pattern.

Flower Aesthetics

Bloomcore, also known as Floral, is an aesthetic based on theme of flowers, gardens, gardening, and nature. Though generally similar, Bloomcore differs from Naturecore in its focus on village greenery, emphasis of flowers, and themes of a quiet lifestyle.

Basic Flower Arrangement

A flower arrangement is a group of flowers designed into a container, which may be displayed in; a vase, box, ceramic, basket or container. The flowers are arranged into a wet floral foam, which holds water, much like a sponge.
